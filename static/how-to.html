how-to
0. requirements
- anki desktop running
- ankiconnect add-on installed (port 8765)
- obsidian vault with class folders (ex: math250, phys202)
1. format your notes
Use this syntax inside your markdown files: 
Q:: what is the derivative of x^2?
A:: 2x
Answers can span multiple lines after the A:: line.
2. launch ankivert
Start the server, then open the UI in your browser: 
ankivert
Visit http://127.0.0.1:8000.
3. paste your vault path
Example path: 
~/Documents/Obsidian_vault/Obsidian/101_CerritosCollege/03_sp26
4. scan or sync
- scan: preview cards without adding to anki
- sync: add new cards to anki
- dry-run: simulate sync without adding
